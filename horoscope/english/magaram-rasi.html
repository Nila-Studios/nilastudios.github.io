Magaram (Capricorn)

Magaram Rasi, known as Capricorn in Western astrology, is the tenth sign of the zodiac. It is represented by the Crocodile (or Mountain Goat) and is ruled by Saturn (Sani), the planet of discipline and karma. As an Earth sign, Magaram natives are practical, ambitious, and grounded.

Personality

Individuals born in Magaram Rasi are the most disciplined and hardworking of the zodiac. They possess a serious demeanor and a strong sense of responsibility. Their personality is characterized by perseverance and a practical approach to life. They are natural planners who look at the long-term consequences of their actions. They value tradition and respect authority, often becoming pillars of stability in their families and workplaces.

Strengths

The primary strength of Magaram is their unwavering determination. They have the patience to climb the highest mountains to achieve their goals. Their practical nature makes them excellent at managing resources and time. They are highly reliable and can be trusted with significant responsibilities. Their disciplined lifestyle and ability to stay calm under pressure are key to their success.

Weaknesses

On the downside, their serious nature can sometimes make them appear cold or unemotional. Magaram natives can be prone to pessimism if things don't go according to plan. They can be very stubborn and find it difficult to change their mind once it's set. Their focus on work and goals can sometimes lead to them neglecting their emotional and social life. They may also be overly critical of themselves and others.

Career

Magaram individuals excel in careers that require structure, organization, and long-term planning. They make excellent administrators, managers, and government officials. Their practical and financial skills often lead them to success in banking, accounting, and real estate. They also thrive in engineering, architecture, and law. They prefer careers with a clear hierarchy and the potential for steady advancement.

Marriage

In marriage, Magaram Rasi people are extremely loyal and seek a partner who is equally responsible and stable. They view marriage as a serious commitment and work hard to provide security for their family. They are most compatible with fellow Earth signs like Rishabam (Taurus) and Kanni (Virgo). They also share a good bond with Viruchigam (Scorpio) and Meenam (Pisces) due to their shared values of loyalty and depth.

Lucky Color

The lucky colors for Magaram are Blue, Black, and Dark Green. These colors resonate with the disciplined and stable energy of Saturn.

Lucky Number

The lucky numbers for Magaram natives are 8, 17, and 26. Number 8 is particularly auspicious as it is associated with Saturn.

Lucky Stone

Blue Sapphire (Neelam) is the recommended lucky stone for Magaram. Wearing it can help enhance their discipline, health, and professional success, provided it suits their horoscope.
